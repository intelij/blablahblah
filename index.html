Basattie Farms

In Hayzview, Mpumalanga Highveld, South Africa, the farming operation was established in the year 2014 by the Thebe Family with the vision of establishing the family roots on a rural land that they can call their own, whilst developing a sustainable farming enterprise on the land.

About Us

Basattie Farms engages in intensive farming throughout its operations in an effort of utilising the full productive capacity of the land. Operations range from animal farming, which includes beef cattle farming, cattle feedlot, sheep rearing for meat and preserving a apple orchard. Basattie Farms produces the following crops: maize, soya beans and hay.

Our Different Departments

From cattle (beef) farming and sheep farming, Basattie Farms focuses largely on implementing sound managerial practices in order to increase productivity when it comes to their livestock. Basattie Farms has been dedicated to implementing only the best farming practices when it comes to their crop farming, which has maize, soya beans and hay.
